Luis Fernando Díaz Marulanda (Barrancas, La Guajira, January 13, 1997) is a Colombian footballer who plays as a winger and his current team is Liverpool of the English Premier League. He is international with the Colombian National Team.

'Lucho' Díaz as he is nicknamed, very young arrived at the field where they were choosing 22 soccer players to represent the national team of the Colombian indigenous team in the contest, the soccer player just touching the ball and making two dribbles discredited the coaching staff of the Pibe Valderrama and John Jairo "Pocillo" Díaz, who chose him as one of their best players for the tournament.
